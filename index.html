Since PureRate is an open-source tool distributed outside the App Store without Apple's paid signature, macOS applies a "quarantine" flag that may trigger a "damaged" error.

Simple Fix:
  1. Drag PureRate to your Applications folder.
  2. Open Terminal.app (Command + Space, type Terminal).
  3. Paste this command and press Enter:
    xattr -cr /Applications/PureRate.app

This removes the internet quarantine flag and allows the app to run perfectly. Alternatively, you can go to System Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway".
